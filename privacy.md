# Privacy Policy for Long-Form

Last updated: 2025-12-17

Long-Form is developed by NateDevJ. This app helps you save, organize and track links to YouTube videos, podcasts and books.

## Data Collection and Storage

- Long-Form **does not collect, transmit or store any personal data on external servers**.
- All data you enter in the app (media items, notes, tags, categories, progress) is stored **locally on your device** using Apple’s storage technologies (UserDefaults/App Group container).
- When you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed from your device.

## Third-Party Content

- The app allows you to save links to **third-party content** such as YouTube videos, podcasts and books.
- For convenience, Long-Form may display **titles, thumbnails and metadata** retrieved from these services.
- All third-party content remains hosted by the original providers. Long-Form does not claim any rights to this content.

## Analytics and Tracking

- Long-Form **does not use any third-party analytics, tracking SDKs or advertising frameworks**.
- The app does not create user profiles and does not track you across apps or websites.

## Network Access

- Network access is used only to fetch metadata (such as titles, thumbnails or duration) for the links you provide.
- These requests are sent directly to the respective services (e.g. YouTube) and are used solely to display information inside the app.

## Children’s Privacy

Long-Form is not directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
